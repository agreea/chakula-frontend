Amazing Homecooked Meals at Georgetown.

We're on a mission to create a new dining option for Georgetown students: each other's cooking.

Why?

It's surprisingly hard for a lot of students to get a good homecooked meal. And loneliness hits us all more often than we want to admit.

Chakula's out to fix that.

We want to bring homecooked group meals back into Georgetown students' lives. The food is awesome (we taste test every chef) and the you, the people, are even awesomer. Join the movement. Let's make magic together.
