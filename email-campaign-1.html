Allow us to introduce ourselves

Chakula started because Pat, a junior without a meal plan, had been reduced to a diet of frozen pizza while Agree, a recent transfer, felt lonely and missed cooking for others. From a dinner cooked one Sunday, a weekly tradition started that would feed hundreds of hungry stomachs over nearly 3 years. The food was great and the conversations were unforgettable. After many of our friends wanted to start their own weekly dinners, we began to ask, "What would it take to bring this idea to the rest of Georgetown?"

So we came up with Chakula. It's a way for you to find delicious homecooked meals, lovingly made by Georgetown's best chefs. For the price of a burrito and chips, you can get an unforgettable experience over incredible food.

We want to feed Hoyas food they can't find at restaurants or dining halls. We want to build communities around dinner tables. And maybe if it works, Georgetown will feel just a little more like home.

Agree & Pat
